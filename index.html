How Digital Evidence Helps Investigations

Computer forensics, also known as digital forensics, is the process of collecting, analyzing, and tracing digital evidence from electronic devices. This evidence can come from phones, laptops, emails, hard drives, cloud accounts, and online platforms.

Why This Topic Matters

Technology has become a huge part of everyday life. Since people use electronic devices so often, these devices can hold important information for investigations. Phones and computers can reveal call logs, text messages, emails, internet history, login information, and location records.

Digital evidence can help investigators connect online activity to real events.
