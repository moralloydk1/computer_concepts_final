How Digital Evidence Helps Investigations

Computer forensics, also known as digital forensics, is the process of collecting, analyzing, and tracing digital evidence from electronic devices. This evidence can come from phones, laptops, emails, hard drives, cloud accounts, and online platforms.

Why Computer Forensics Matters

Now that technology has become a major part of everyday life, it's important to realize how crucial technology can be in investigations. People carry around phones, computers, tablets, cloud backups, and more very often on their day-to-day activities. These devices can actually hold important information for investigations. Computer forensics helps investigators connect digital activity to real life.

What Can Be Found?

  • Internet and search history
  • Emails
  • Call logs
  • Text messages
  • Location records
  • Login information
  • Files
