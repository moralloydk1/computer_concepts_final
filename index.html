Computer Forensics

Computer forensics combines both legal forensics and computer science in order to find, collect, and study digital evidence from computers, phones, and other electronic devices. This process is used to gather digital evidence that is usable in the court of law.

What This Website Covers

This website explains what computer forensics is, how deleted data can sometimes be recovered, and why computer forensics is important in today's digital world.
