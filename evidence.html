What Is Digital Evidence?

Digital evidence is information that is stored or transmitted digitally that can be used in court. It can be found on phones, computers, hard drives, flash drives, cameras, online accounts, cloud storage, and more.

Examples of Digital Evidence

  • Text messages and emails
  • Call logs
  • Photos and videos
  • Browser history
  • Location records
  • Files and deleted files
  • Social media activity

Why It Is Important

Digital evidence can help investigators understand what happened in a case. It may show where someone was, who they communicated with, or what activity happened on a device.
